How AI-Powered Welding Helmets Are Redefining Worker Safety

ZK
Zeekers R&D Team
 May 2025
 8 min read

Developed by Zeekers Technology Solutions Pvt. Ltd. with support from Garden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers Limited (GRSE), ZweldMET is an AI-powered Smart Welding Helmet designed to provide intelligent welding path guidance and real-time welding parameter monitoring. Successfully validated during industrial trials at GRSE, Kolkata, the system demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can improve welding accuracy, process monitoring, and the future of smart manufacturing.
