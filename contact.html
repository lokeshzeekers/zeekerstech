FAQ
Common Questions
Answers to the questions we get asked most often. Still have one? Use the form above.
How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
We aim to respond to all email enquiries within 24 hours on business days (Mon–Sat). For urgent technical support or critical production issues, we recommend calling us directly during office hours for the fastest response.
Can I visit the Zeekers R&D lab?
Yes, visits to our Coimbatore R&D facility are welcome — but they must be scheduled in advance. Please email us at sales@zeekerstech.com with your name, organisation, and the purpose of your visit, and we'll arrange a convenient time.
Do you offer on-site product demonstrations?
Yes. For enterprise clients and institutions, we can arrange on-site demonstrations at your facility. Use the "Request Demo" tab in the contact form above and select "On-site Demo" as your preferred format. We will follow up to schedule at your convenience.
How do I set up an AICTE-IDEA Lab at my institution?
Contact us via the "General Enquiry" form and select "IDEA Lab Setup" as your inquiry type. Our education partnerships team will reach out to discuss your institution's requirements, funding eligibility under AICTE, equipment selection, and the complete setup process.
Can I request a product brochure or technical specification sheet?
Yes — product brochures and technical datasheets for all ZTS products are available on our Products page via the "Download Brochure" button on each product listing. For detailed specifications, integration guides, or API documentation, please contact our technical team at helpdesk@zeekerstech.com.
Are you open to research collaborations or academic partnerships?
Absolutely. Research collaboration is central to how ZTS operates — it's what led to our IDEX-backed partnership with GRSE Ltd. If your institution or research group is working on AI, IoT, welding technology, or industrial automation, we'd love to explore how we can work together. Reach out via the form above.
How do I apply for an internship or job at Zeekers?
Visit our Careers page to see all open positions and apply directly through the application form. For internship enquiries not listed on the Careers page, you can also send your CV and a brief note about your interest to hr@zeekerstech.com.
