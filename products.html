ZwelDAQ

AI-Integrated Welding Data Acquisition & Quality Assessment System

A cost-effective IoT solution designed for welding machines with integrated AI-based weld quality assessment. Unlike expensive OEM systems available only on select models, ZwelDAQ can be integrated with any welding machine using simple arrangements. The device captures critical welding parameters in real time for analyzing welding performance, machine utilization, and welder productivity.

0.5ms Sampling
128GB Data Logging
WiFi Enabled
99.5% Accuracy
1 Year Warranty
Basic Version — Measures welding current and voltage with WiFi connectivity, along with options for job card entry and worker details. Machine utilization and welding data are securely stored in the cloud for future analysis.
Prime Version — Adds monitoring of wire feed rate and gas flow rate while continuing to track machine utilization, electrical parameters, and operator data through cloud connectivity.
Universal Integration — Compatible with almost any welding machine without expensive OEM dependencies, making advanced welding analytics accessible and portable.

Zeeclean

Autonomous Cleaning Robot for Poultry Sheds & Farms

Zeeclean is an autonomous cleaning robot purpose-built for poultry farms, designed to automate the removal of droppings, feathers, and litter debris from shed floors. Powered by AI-based navigation and sensor fusion, Zeeclean moves through poultry sheds on a scheduled or on-demand basis, improving hygiene, reducing manual labour, and supporting better biosecurity and flock health across poultry operations.

Autonomous Shed Navigation — Uses sensor fusion to navigate poultry shed floors around feeders, drinkers, and equipment without manual guidance.
Hygiene-First Cleaning — Systematically clears droppings, feathers, and litter debris to maintain cleaner shed conditions between full litter changes.
Built for Poultry Operations — Designed specifically for poultry farms and processing sheds, reducing manual cleaning labour and supporting consistent biosecurity standards.
